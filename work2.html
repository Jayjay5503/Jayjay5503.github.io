MOUNT
FUJI

Mount Fuji is the highest mountain in Japan, standing 3,776 meters tall. Mount Fuji lies about 100 kilometers southwest of Tokyo and is visible from the city on clear days.

It is one of Japan's "Three Holy Mountains". Mount Fuji has been a popular destination for tourists and mountain climbers alike.

KYOTO,
JAPAN

Kyoto is a city on the island of Honshu, Japan. It was the capital of Japan for over a thousand years and is known for its classical Buddhist temples, as well as gardens, imperial palaces, and Shinto shrines.

Notable sites include the Golden Pavilion, Fushimi Inari-taisha, and Arashiyama Bamboo Grove.

OSAKA,
JAPAN

Osaka is a large port city and commercial center on the Japanese island of Honshu. It is known for modern architecture, nightlife, and hearty street food. The city has a rich history that dates back centuries.

Osaka Castle is a famous landmark, while Universal Studios Japan is one of the city's popular attractions.
