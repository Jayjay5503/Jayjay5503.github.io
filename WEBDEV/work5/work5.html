•MAKE • YOUR • MOMMA • PROUD•

MYMP (Make Your Momma Proud) is a popular
Filipino acoustic band, originally formed in 2003.
The band gained widespread recognition with
its smooth acoustic style and soulful renditions
of pop and R&B songs.
The band’s music often features romantic themes,
making it a favorite in the acoustic and
pop music scene in the Philippines.
